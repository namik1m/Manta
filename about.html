Creativity Towards You

This is MANTA media - an expanding network of offbeat creative professionals. Through combining our skills and love for image and sound we create visuals, audios and concepts that go beyond limits. Founded in 2017 by Davids Danoss, MANTA started off by working with other creatives - artists, performers, musicians, etc. But as the time goes, MANTA is growing over entrepreneurship and commerce offering creative solutions to anyone that comes its way.. We are excited, driven and ready for action!

What We Do

Fast paced workflow, cost-efficient creations and all-around service is MANTA way of working. We combine high-end gear with pro-level skills to do the audio-visuals for you - from simple, small scale to big-budget productions. We are here to make it happen! Tell us your idea and we will provide the best solution done by the amazing creatives from our network. See our work and get inspired by MANTA collaborations with theaters, art organizations, festivals, individual entrepreneurs and corporations.
